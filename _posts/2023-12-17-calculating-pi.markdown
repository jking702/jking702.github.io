An interesting idea came to me recently when I was watching the film [pi][pi-imdb]. In the film the reclusive mathematical genius Max Cohen discovers a 216 digit long number that becomes of interest to religious sects and stock brokers alike, as this number has been shown to accurately predict day to day stock prices and apparently has some links to the true name of god.

Regardless of the existance of such a number, the number apparently had links to the mathematical constant we all know about, and consequently the title of the film, pi. So it made me start thinking about how we calculate pi as it has no real patterns in it.

So without doing much research I thought I might try and calculate pi to at least 20 decimal places, for no other reason than that it sounds like a lot.
